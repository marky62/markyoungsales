# Mark Young Sales — Claude Skills

This folder contains five project-specific Claude skills for the Mark Young
Sales business. Each skill encodes the rules, frameworks, decisions, and
context needed to produce consistent work across sessions without having
to re-explain everything from scratch.

Install these `.skill` files in any Claude environment (Cowork, Claude Code)
that needs access to them.

---

## Skills

### `mys-ideation.skill`
**When to use:** Generating new video ideas, developing a rough concept,
evaluating whether an idea suits the channel, or planning the content pipeline.

Contains: The full idea bank (all developed post-launch ideas), the five
evaluation filters for video ideas, the brief format for handing off to
title/thumbnail work, and content pipeline principles.

---

### `mys-title-thumbnail.skill`
**When to use:** Writing a video title, generating thumbnail text, evaluating
a title against BENS, deciding whether to repackage an underperforming video.

Contains: The five locked videos as reference patterns, the full 6-step
title and thumbnail process, BENS evaluation framework, thumbnail rules,
repackaging decision logic, and all standing copy rules.

---

### `mys-yt-scripting.skill`
**When to use:** Writing or reviewing a YouTube script, improving a hook,
structuring the body of a video, writing a CTA, or checking a script
against MYS standards.

Contains: Pre-writing and post-writing checklists, the four-section
structure (hook, body, bridge, CTA), setup/payoff thread guidance,
the brick system (transition → emotion → logic), writing rules, and
Notion save protocol.

---

### `mys-vod-scripting.skill`
**When to use:** Writing or reviewing a VOD module script, adding a new
section to an existing part, writing supplemental material explanations,
or extending the 90-Day Account Plan course content.

Contains: The four-part course structure, Sarah Mitchell's fixed figures,
section structure guidance, tone reference from locked scripts,
supplemental download reference table, and Notion page IDs for all four parts.

---

### `mys-vsl-scripting.skill`
**When to use:** Writing or revising a VSL script, developing the three
secrets structure, editing the hook or final choice, preparing a new VSL
for a different offer, or getting a script ready for filming.

Contains: The domino statement, the three false beliefs, the locked
12-step structure, the Ali's Bins principle, writing rules, filming notes,
and version control protocol.

---

## Notes

- Skills are read-only reference material — Claude reads them at the start
  of relevant tasks and follows the instructions inside.
- If a skill needs updating (new locked decisions, changed figures, new
  rules), edit the SKILL.md inside the relevant `.skill` file and
  re-package it.
- All five skills reference the Mark Young Sales Notion workspace for
  saving approved work. Notion page IDs are encoded directly in the
  relevant skills.
